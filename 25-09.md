### [I WISH](http://iteslj.org/questions/doyouwish.html)
Utilizar o `I WISH + PAST SIMPLE` para desejar coisas no presente

- I wish to lived in another country

- Do you wish studied in a different university? 
  **R:** I wish i studied in a different university

- Do you wish you were fashinable?
  **R:** Yes, I wish i were.

- I wish i went parachuting.
- I wish i skydove.

**NUNCA** utilizar `was` como auxiliar nesta estrutura!

- I wish she **were** rich.

- I wish i **were** a mega sena winner.

## Commom sentences

### How come
Podemos utilizar `how come` no lugar de `why`.

- **How come** you are sad?

- **How come** you don't know what to do today?

### Make sure
Utilizado como `ter certeza`.

- **Make sure** the cats are feed.

### No need
Utilizado como `Não há motivo`

- There is **no need** to worry about it.

### No use
Utilizado como `não adianta`

It **is no use** to cry.
There **is no use** studing a lot in Brazil, you don't even find a job anyway.
